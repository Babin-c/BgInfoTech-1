What services does BG InfoTechs offer?

We provide comprehensive digital solutions including website development, mobile apps, social media marketing, digital marketing campaigns, IT training programs, web hosting services, and graphic design - all tailored for Nepalese businesses.

How long does it take to develop a website?

Typical website projects take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Basic brochure sites can be completed in 10-15 days, while e-commerce solutions may require 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! All our projects come with 1 month free support. We offer affordable maintenance packages that include regular updates, security checks, and technical support. Our team is always available to help our clients in Dhangadhi and across Nepal.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, Khalti, eSewa, and cash payments. For larger projects, we typically require 30% advance payment, 40% upon midpoint completion, and the remaining 30% after final delivery and testing.

Can you help with digital marketing for my local business?

Absolutely! We specialize in helping Nepalese businesses grow online. Our digital marketing services include Facebook/Instagram ads, Google Ads, SEO optimization, and content marketing - all customized for your local target audience in the Far-Western region.
